Find an utterance in Utterances

Usage

uttnum(utt, str)

Arguments

utt A mu+ utterance vector.
str The full name of the utterance.

Value

An integer, corresponding to the index of the utterance in the Utterances vector.

See Also

mkdb

Examples

 uttnum(my.utts, "/mu/mu/MUDEMO/msajc001")
